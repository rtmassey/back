Back Exercise Reference Guide

Daily Exercises
1.
Dead Bug With Marching / Femur Arc
  • Lie on your back with legs up, knees and hips bent at 90°
  • Keep your low back neutral or flat against the surface throughout
  • Brace your abdominals and slowly lower one leg toward the surface
  • Lightly tap the foot to the surface and return to starting position
  • Repeat with the other leg, maintaining a 90° bend in both knees
  • Keep a steady breathing pattern throughout the exercise
Repeat: 5 times  ·  Complete: 2 sets  ·  Perform: 1 time per day
2.
Prone Hip Extension
  • Lie face down on the floor with your legs straight
  • Lift your belly button off the floor and press your pelvis into the floor
  • Raise one thigh up while keeping your leg straight and engaging your buttocks
  • Lower your leg slowly. Repeat on the other side.
Repeat: 10 times  ·  Hold: 2 seconds  ·  Complete: 1 set  ·  Perform: 1 time per day
3.
Bird/Dog
  • Start on hands and knees (quadruped position)
  • Keep shoulders above hands, hips above knees, head neutral and relaxed
  • Engage your core and remember to breathe
  • Extend one arm, one leg, or opposite arm/leg
  • Keep movement slow and controlled to prevent hip rotation
  • Progress: Level 1 (one arm) → Level 2 (one leg) → Level 3 (opposite arm/leg)
  • Purpose: Develop functional core strength
Repeat: 10 times  ·  Hold: 2 seconds  ·  Complete: 1 set  ·  Perform: 3 times per week
4.
Open Book
  • Lie on your side with arms straight out, hands pressed together in front of you
  • Knees are pulled up and bent towards your chest
  • Turn your torso so your back lies flat on the surface, arms open like a book
  • Hold this position before returning to the starting position
Repeat: 10 times  ·  Hold: 1 second  ·  Perform: 1 time per day
5.
Lower Trunk Rotations (LTR) – Wig Wags – Knee Rocks
  • Lie on your back with your knees bent
  • Gently rotate your spine as you move your knees to one side
  • Reverse directions and move your knees to the other side
  • Repeat as you move through a comfortable range of motion
Repeat: 10 times  ·  Hold: 1 second  ·  Complete: 1 set  ·  Perform: 1 time per day
Exercises 3 Times Per Week
6.
Single Knee to Chest Stretch 2 (SKTC)
  • Lie on your back
  • Use your hands to gently lift one knee and pull it toward your chest
  • Hold for a gentle stretch
  • Keep your other knee straight and resting on the surface
Repeat: 2 times  ·  Hold: 20 seconds  ·  Complete: 1 set  ·  Perform: 1 time per day
7.
March Kickout
  • Lie on your back with both knees bent
  • Tighten your core by drawing your bellybutton toward your spine, flattening your back against the surface
  • Hold this contraction as you slowly extend one leg, return to neutral, then extend the opposite leg
  • Keep your back flat the whole time — the lower your leg hovers, the more challenging
Repeat: 10 times  ·  Complete: 1 set  ·  Perform: 3 times per week
8.
Dead Bug With Arm Arc
  • Lie on your back with legs up, knees and hips bent at 90°
  • Keep your low back neutral or flat against the surface throughout
  • Point your arms straight up towards the ceiling
  • Exhale as you bring your arms above your head
  • Brace your abdominals to keep your low back neutral or flat
  • Keep a steady breathing pattern throughout the exercise
Repeat: 10 times  ·  Hold: 1 second  ·  Complete: 1 set  ·  Perform: 3 times per week
Always listen to your body and stop if you experience pain. Consult your healthcare provider with any questions.
